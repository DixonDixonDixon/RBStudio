About the Bones

Rare Bones Studio is the independent one-person practice of Dickson Wong — a brand and packaging design director with a decade of experience and a soft spot for work that’s quietly clever, visually sharp and just a little off-centre.

After years inside the branding world, one thing became obvious: in an era of noise, scroll, hot takes and overdesigned everything, people crave clarity and common sense. Rare Bones exists to deliver exactly that — strategic, straight-talking design with strong foundations and zero fluff.

I work directly with founders, distillers and small teams, and I also slot seamlessly into creative departments that need additional senior design capacity on brand and packaging briefs.

It’s design with backbone: intentional, thoughtfully structured, and with just enough bite to be remembered.
