About the Bones

Rare Bones Studio is the independent practice of Dickson — a graphic designer with a decade of experience and a soft spot for work that’s quietly clever, visually sharp, and just a little off-centre.

After years in branding, one thing has become clear: in a world full of noise, hot takes and overdesigned everything, people are drawn to clarity and common sense. Rare Bones exists to deliver exactly that — bold, strategic branding with strong foundations and zero fluff.

We build brands that last: structured, intentional, and with just enough bite to actually be remembered.
