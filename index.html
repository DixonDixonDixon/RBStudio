About the Bones

Rare Bones Studio is the brainchild of Dickson — a graphic designer with a decade of experience and a love for everything beautiful, clever, and just a little offbeat.

After years in the branding industry, one thing has become clear: in an era full of noise, uncertainty, and overcomplication, people crave clarity and common sense. Rare Bones was created to deliver exactly that — bold, strategic branding with strong foundations and zero fluff.

We’re here to help you build the kind of brand that lasts — with structure, intent, and just enough bite to stand out.
