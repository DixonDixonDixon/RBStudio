THE LADDER

Perfection in wine is elusive, almost mythical.
Yet we climb, stay curious, and reach higher each season.
Every step brings us closer to the extraordinary — guided by the land, the moon, and the quiet pursuit of better.

The Ladder is a Crozes-Hermitage wine brand for people who want their everyday glass to rise a little higher. We farm biodynamically and work with the rhythm of the seasons and the moon. It’s a hands-on, labour-heavy way to grow grapes, but it’s how we like to make wine: honest, alive, and thoughtfully made. We’re young, curious, and always aiming higher—one step, one vintage at a time.
