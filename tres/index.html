Tres—

Tres— is a Finnish gin, distilled and macerated with 100% Finnish botanicals. It draws from Sky (spruce tips, birch, heather), Land (angelica, caraway), and Sea (sea buckthorn, samphire) for bright lift, firm structure, and a clean, mineral edge—uncomplicated and unapologetically Finnish.

The design tells a simple story: when three Finnish ingredient groups come together in true harmony, they both defy expectations of how gin can taste and preserve gin’s integrity—so Tres slips into your favourite drinks with an unapologetically Finnish flair.
